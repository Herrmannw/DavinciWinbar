The lounge

Premium seated hospitality, built around wine education and conversation.

Da Vinci's is a boutique wine lounge offering on-premise wine, beer, and limited alcoholic beverage service in a polished, relaxed setting.

The experience centers on guided tastings, social gatherings, integrated retail wine sales, and tables made for a slower evening.

Wine and light bites

Curated bottles, artisan provisions, and easy pairings.

Expect curated retail bottles, wine accessories, gift items, and gourmet specialty provisions alongside a seated lounge menu.

  • Wine by the glass and guided pours
  • Beer and limited alcoholic beverage service
  • Artisan cheese and charcuterie boards
  • Crostini, small gourmet foods, and wine gifts

Tastings and gatherings

A quiet, upscale room for learning, sharing, and discovering.

Wine education and guided tastings will shape the calendar, with thoughtful service for neighborhood gatherings and intimate celebrations.

Retail wine sales are planned to begin at 3:00 PM each operating day, giving guests a chance to take home select bottles and wine-related gifts.
